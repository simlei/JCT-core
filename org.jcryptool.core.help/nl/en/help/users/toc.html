User Guide

JCrypTool (JCT) is a free and open-source cryptographic e-learning software with many visualizations.

It is based on the Eclipse Rich Client Platform (RCP). You will recognize a lot of concepts in case you ever worked with Eclipse or an Eclipse RCP.

Beginner or not, the User Guide will introduce you in the many possibilities you have with JCrypTool. The following pages therefore contain information about the different Editors, Views and Perspectives. And of course the crypto plug-ins, starting from Algorithms, Cryptanalysis, Games, and last but not least Visualizations.

The content of the User Guide (online help) is delivered by the developers of the crypto plug-ins. This means that one plug-in contains more help, the other one less. The JCT core team tries to ensure a minimum and consistent quality over all plug-ins and their according online help. In case you do have any question about a certain plug-in or need more assistance it is best to contact the plug-in developer directly. In case you do not know the developer or you cannot reach him, feel free to post a message in the JCrypTool Developer Chat.

The online help for the JCrypTool core is fully available in German and English.

Your feedback (bugs, ideas, and wishes) is very welcome, no matter whether it's for a core or a crypto plug-in. Feel free to post to our JCrypTool Issue Tracker or within the JCrypTool Developer Gitter Chat. For the Issue Tracker you need a GitHub account, for the Gitter chat you need either a Twitter, a GitLab or a GitHub account.
